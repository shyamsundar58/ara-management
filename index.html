VISION AND GOALS

Vision

To empower athletes in Saudi Arabia by creating a sustainable ecosystem where they can thrive and inspire through their passion, while bridging the gap between sports and strategic corporate investments.

Goal

To support athletes in achieving their fullest potential while providing businesses with targeted, high-impact marketing opportunities in the rapidly growing Saudi sports industry.

Long-Term Objective

To become the leading sports management company in the region, driving athlete success and maximizing returns for investors through strategic partnerships and innovative initiatives aligned with Saudi Vision 2030.

At ARA Sports Management, we are athletes first and foremost. Founded in Saudi Arabia, our company was born out of a desire to empower athletes to pursue their passions and reach their full potential. We recognized a critical gap in the market—athletes who are committed to excellence are often underpaid and unable to dedicate themselves fully to their craft. With Saudi Vision 2030 placing significant emphasis on investing in health, wellness, and sports, we see a unique opportunity to bridge this gap and create a sustainable future for athletes.

Our mission is twofold:

Empowering Athletes:

We provide athletes with the resources, support, and sponsorship opportunities they need to succeed at both national and international levels. By enabling them to focus on what they love, we aim to cultivate a thriving sports culture in Saudi Arabia.

Delivering Value for Businesses:

We connect forward-thinking companies with a rapidly growing market of athletes who have loyal, engaged fan bases. Sponsoring athletes isn’t just about philanthropy—it’s about strategic, targeted marketing that drives results. By investing in athletes, companies can amplify their brand visibility, engage with passionate communities, and align themselves with the energy and momentum of Saudi Arabia’s transformation into a global sports hub.

Current Market
Market Growth
The market value of Saudi Arabia's sports industry is projected to surge from $8 billion to $22.4 billion by 2030. This expansion is anticipated to contribute $16.5 billion annually to the country's GDP and create over 100,000 jobs, underscoring the sector's role in economic diversification and employment generation.
Audience Engagement and Participation
Public engagement in sports has markedly increased. A survey by the General Authority for Statistics revealed that 48.2% of individuals now engage in physical and sporting activities for at least 30 minutes weekly, a significant rise from previous years. This upward trend aligns with the Vision 2030 Quality of Life program, aiming to elevate sports participation to 40% by 2030.
Professional Athletes and International Events
The Kingdom has made strategic investments to enhance its sports profile globally. Additionally, Saudi Arabia has secured hosting rights for major events such as the Saudi Arabian Formula 1 Grand Prix, the Diriyah Formula E Grand Prix, Dakar Rally, WTA Finals, Next Gen Tennis, the 2027 Asian Cup, the 2029 Asian Winter Games, and the 2034 FIFA World Cup, positioning itself as a burgeoning hub for international sports.
Corporate Investment
Corporate Investments and Sponsorships
The sports sector's growth has attracted substantial corporate investments. Saudi Arabia has secured over 900 sponsorship agreements, including 194 specifically related to football, reflecting a strategic effort to leverage sports for economic and geopolitical influence.

These partnerships offer companies lucrative opportunities to engage with a rapidly expanding and passionate sports audience.

In summary, Saudi Arabia's sports sector is undergoing transformative growth, marked by increased public participation, substantial economic contributions, the attraction of top-tier professional athletes, and significant corporate investments. These developments highlight the Kingdom's strategic commitment to establishing a dynamic and sustainable sports ecosystem.
